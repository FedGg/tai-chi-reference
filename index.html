The 24-Form — Simplified Taijiquan

Created in 1956 by the Chinese Sports Commission as an introduction to Yang-style Tai Chi. Sometimes called the Beijing form. It distils the traditional form into 24 movements covering all essential principles — taking roughly 5–6 minutes to perform.
